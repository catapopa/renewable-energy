Network Analysis Concepts

Community

A community is a group of nodes that are more densely connected to each other than to the rest of the network, revealing patterns among locations.

PageRank

PageRank measures the importance of each location based on its connections, indicating which sites are most influential in the network.

Importance for the Project

In our project, understanding communities helps us identify areas that may benefit from similar renewable energy solutions, especially in regions with similar weather patterns. PageRank allows us to measure how important each location is, helping us find key players in renewable energy. This knowledge leads to better decision-making about where to invest in projects, ultimately creating a more sustainable future.
